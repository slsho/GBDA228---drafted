About Me!

Sophia is a 2A student in GBDA who has multiple different interests in the digital art landscape. She hopes to work for her dream company in the future, which is Riot Games. She currently enjoys playing around with graphic design, nail art and app creation. From young she has had an interest in computer science and started coding when she started university. She is currently pursueing a computing minor with the University of Waterloo!


Sophia's end goal is to fluent in coding, digital visual art (animation, illustation and graphic design), and business. To work towards her goal as a game designer.
